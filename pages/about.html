Our Story

About Beacon of Hope

A registered non-profit organisation dedicated to caring for orphaned and vulnerable children with love, dignity, and purpose.

Who We Are

We Care. We Love.

Beacon of Hope (NPO 307-989) is a community-driven non-profit organisation committed to making a meaningful difference in the lives of orphaned and vulnerable children (OVC) and their families.

We believe that every child, regardless of their circumstances, deserves love, care, and the opportunity to thrive. Our dedicated team of caregivers and coordinators works tirelessly to ensure that each beneficiary receives the attention, documentation, and support they need.

From regular home visits and file management to community outreach and beneficiary recruitment, Beacon of Hope is present in the daily lives of the children we serve.

Our Vision & Mission
🏡

Home-Based Care

Caregivers visit beneficiaries in their homes, building trust and gathering the information needed to support each child effectively.

📋

Professional Records

Every beneficiary has a dedicated file with full documentation, history, and process notes maintained by our coordinators.

🤝

Community Accountability

We hold regular meetings, maintain attendance registers, and ensure every volunteer is committed and accountable.

❤️

Family Support

Beyond children, we support their families and communities, addressing root causes and building resilience together.
