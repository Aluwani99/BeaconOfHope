What We Do

Serving the Most Vulnerable

We walk alongside orphaned and vulnerable children, their families, and communities to build a foundation of love and dignity.

Home Visits

Caregivers visit beneficiaries at home to assess needs, collect documents, and record full background histories for each child in our care.

File & Record Management

Every beneficiary's information is carefully filed and maintained by our coordinators to ensure accurate, accessible records for each case.

Beneficiary Recruitment

We actively identify and recruit orphaned and vulnerable children into our programme, ensuring no child falls through the cracks of the system.

Community Support

Beyond individual children, we uplift the broader community through caregiver training, mutual support, and collaborative fundraising.

Every Rand Makes a Difference

Your contribution directly supports home visits, file management, and the daily care of vulnerable children in our community.
